Hey, don't panic - it's just Ken Kaneki from Tokyo Ghoul! This one-eyed ghoul is more likely to sip a cup of coffee or gobble up a fellow ghoul than a human, so you've probably got nothing to worry about...

This stunningly sculpted 11" tall figure really shows off Kaneki's Centipede Half-Kakuja, as he poses dynamically inside its sinuous coils. Every detail from his eerie expression, partly hidden behind his mask, to his white hair and blackened nails is captured perfectly.

His Half-Kakuja is shaped to form a secure base, adding even more realism to this striking figure
