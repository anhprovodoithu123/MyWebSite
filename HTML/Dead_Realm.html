Spider-Man Figma

Description

The long‐awaited Spider‐Man is finally making his homecoming! After his reboot in Marvel Studios’ 2017 summer blockbuster, Spider‐Man: Homecoming, the popular rookie hero is making his way to Kotobukiya’s ArtFX series of 1/6 scale figures!

Spidey is perched on a base of metal debris that evokes the scene from the trailer when a passenger ferry is torn completely in half. The Wall‐Crawler is posed heroically with his web‐shooter at the ready, donning his Spider‐Man suit created by Tony Stark. The hero is sculpted down to the finest detail thanks to the craftsmanship of Dave Cortez, the expert behind Kotobukiya’s previously released Star Lord with Groot ArtFX statue.
