Vampyr

Description

Survive and fight against Vampyr hunters, undead skals, and other supernatural creatures. Use your unholy powers to manipulate and delve into the lives of those around you, to decide who will be your next victim.

System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64 bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7 3770 3.4 GHz or AMD AMD FX-8350 4.0 GHz
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 2GB or AMD Radeon R7 370.
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 20 GB available space
  • Sound Card: DirectX 9.0c compatible sound card
  • Additional Notes: At least 2 GB DDR Video Memory
